ABOUT US

Our mission is to create a safe space where you can talk about your experiences with menstruation. Be it the story of your first period, a health issue that you encountered and want to talk about, or a general opinion on various other conversations around menstruation, you now have a place to voice your concerns. We tend to push these conversations under the rug and even when we do talk about this it is often forgotten that it is an experience shared not only by cis women but also other members of the LGBTIQA+ community. Keeping this in mind we also have a section dedicated to non binary people to share their period stories.
